20" Bio


I started off as a neuropsychologist - It was not a fit for me though. After teaching English for a while, I worked in organizational design and process improvement. Realizing that great organizations are the result of people and their behavior , I jumped to leadership development. As I ran programs all over the world, I became more and more interested innovation and technology. I decided to learn computer science and do work at the crossroads of all my favourite things.
